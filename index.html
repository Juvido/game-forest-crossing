Forest Crossing Adventure

A journey through the Elvan Forest, where strange, winding trails wind their way down into eerie depths. Who knows what monstrous creatures we might encounter along the way?

Your mission is to cross Elvan and deliver the legendary Stone Hammer to pacify and protect the people of Stonebridge from an ancient curse

There are safe paths to cross the Elvan Forest, and you will need to make some attempts to find them. There are passages that make you walk much further or lead directly to death.

May the luck of the gods accompany you on the adventure ahead!
